# How to tell the sex of a kitten

## Source
https://icatcare.org/articles/how-to-tell-the-sex-of-a-kitten

---

## Determining the sex of your kitten isn’t as easy as you might think – especially if they’re under 8 weeks old. So, how can you tell if your kitten is male or female?

## How do I tell the sex of my kitten?

#### When your kitten is relaxed, gently lift their tail. You need to look at two things

1. The space between the anus and the genital opening

2. The shape of the genital opening

In female cats, the anus and vulva openings are close together and look like the letter ‘i’ – this is because the opening to the vulva looks like a line.

In male cats, there’s more space between the anus and the opening to the penis, which looks like a circle. You may be able to see or feel small testicles in this space between the anus and penis, but it’s not always possible, depending on the age of your kitten.

## When can I tell the sex of my kitten?

It’s easier to tell the sex of a kitten over 8 weeks of age, purely because they’ll be more developed by this point, however, it is possible to tell the sex from a younger age.

If you’re not sure of the sex of your kitten, ask your veterinary team to check for you.
